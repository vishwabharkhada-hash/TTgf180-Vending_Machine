## R1
The vending machine will accept online payment only.
[V1 Online Payment Only Validation](validation.md#v1-online-payment-only-validation)

## R2
The vending machine will provide product if payment is fully done.
[V2 Product Dispensing Validation](validation.md#v2-product-dispensing-validation)

## R3
The vending machine shall not support cash, coin, banknote, balance accumulation, or change calculation.
[V3 Cash Payment Removal Validation](validation.md#V3-Cash-Payment-Removal-Validation)

## R4
The vending machine will reset itself if timeout[over 1 min].
[V4 Timeout Reset Validation](validation.md#v4-Timeout-Reset-Validation)

## R5
The vending machine will proceed if user enter the number of product.
[V5 Product Selection Validation](validation.md#V5-Product-Selection-Validation)

## R6
The vending machine shall complete the payment process when online payment is selected and payment success is received.
[V6 Online Payment Validation](validation.md#V6-Online-Payment-Validation)

## R7
If Product is dispensed, the vending machine reset itself. (Rest in State A)
[V7 Automatic Reset After Dispensing Validation](validation.md#V7-Automatic-Reset-After-Dispensing-Validation)

## R8
The vending machine shall support manual reset of the transaction.
[V8 Manual Reset Validation](validation.md#V8-Manual-Reset-Validation)
